Introduction
For the purpose of our assignment, we have analysed the financial reporting practices of Coca-Cola, particularly profitability ratios and the relation thereof with a specific valuation ratio and have found the following results:

- Coca-Cola uses numerical indicators to communicate their financial information to users of the financial reports; and
- it is somewhat difficult to understand the scale and sources of these numerical indicators.

As such, we have considered what can be done to provide a better understanding of this information to the stakeholders of Coca-Cola:

- The first would be the visual presentation of financial information, especially of the information that is relevant to South African investors, creditors and stakeholders of similar categories.
- The second is the clarification of the aspects of the financial information that have been used to calculate the aforementioned financial information.
- The third and final solution is a more personal approach to the explanation of financial information, which can be seen on the following webpage.
